About the College

Food technology education rooted in Rayalaseema and built for industry.

The College of Food Science and Technology, Pulivendula is a constituent college of ANGRAU, established to develop trained human resources for food processing, quality certification, food safety, value addition, and modernized food plants.

Set among sweet orange gardens near Pulivendula, the campus combines classroom learning, laboratory practice, extension awareness, and student activities connected to the needs of farmers, processors, entrepreneurs, and consumers.

Vision

To be a value-based higher education and research institution in food technology, preparing graduates with problem-solving skills and global competitiveness.

Academics

A focused pathway into food science, engineering, and safety.

Students learn through a blend of science fundamentals, food processing principles, product development, quality control, safety standards, and practical exposure to equipment and industry workflows.

B.Tech

Food Technology

A professional undergraduate program designed for careers in processing plants, quality laboratories, food safety, product innovation, entrepreneurship, and higher research.

  • Food processing and preservation
  • Quality assurance and certification
  • Value addition and product development

Facilities

Learning spaces for hands-on food technology training.

Modern Laboratories

State-of-the-art instruments and practical lab sessions support technical learning and product analysis.

Library and Classrooms

Academic spaces are designed for lectures, reference learning, seminars, and collaborative work.

Processing Exposure

Students explore value addition in cereals, pulses, milk, fruits, vegetables, confectionery, and more.

Campus Life

A green campus with academic discipline and community energy.

Student-centered learning

The college environment encourages classroom participation, laboratory confidence, group projects, NSS activities, and career-focused preparation.
